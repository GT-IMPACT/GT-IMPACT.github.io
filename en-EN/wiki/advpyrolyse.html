Advanced Pyrolyse Oven

The principle of operation of a pyrolysis oven is based on the thermal decomposition of organic substances into pyrolysis gases and solid residues.

The advanced Pyrolysis Oven differs from the conventional Pyrolysis Oven тем, in that it is parallel.

Advanced Pyrolyse Oven finished structure

The machine has a unique mechanics - product output during operation.

Principle of operation:


During ("Time", see the picture) of the recipe, gases and solid fuels are released:
  • At 7 seconds - Carbon Monoxide (72L)
  • At 14 seconds - Hydrogen Gas (288L)
  • At 21 seconds - Methane Gas (144L)
  • At 28 seconds - CO2 Gas (216L)
  • At 35 seconds - Wood Tar (1440L) and Charcoal (5)

    • Important!

    The machine, depending on the tier Energy Hatch, performs several recipes at a time (LV - 2x, MV - 4x, HV - 6x .. Tier * 2)

    Energy consumption:

    EU / t = EU / t (prescription) * number of concurrent recipes * Energy Hatch tier / 2

    Example:
    Tier Energy Hatch - MV = 2, which means maximum parallelism = 2 * 2 = 4
    EU/t = 20 * 4 * 2 / 2 = 80 EU/t - provided that you can complete 4 recipes at a time, if you run a recipe 1 at a time, it will be 20 EU/t

    Sawdust pyrolysis

    To build this multiblock you will need 3 types of blocks: HSLA Machine Casing, Steel Pipe Casing and HSLA Frame Box.
    Service hatches (installed anywhere instead of HSLA Machine Casing):
  • Energy Hatch: 1x
  • Maintenance Hatch: 1x
  • Muffler Hatch: 1x
  • Output Bus: до 1x
  • Output Hatch: до 1x
  • Input Bus: 1x
    • The rest of the service hatches are not required.

    To enable the hologram (hint when building), use the Multiblock Machine Blueprint from the TecTech mod, click it on the multiblock controller.

    Important!

    Since it is possible to install only one Output Hatch in the structure, the question arises of how to output all fluids (5 types) from one hatch. It is recommended to use multi-pipes (Nonuple Fluid Pipe).
