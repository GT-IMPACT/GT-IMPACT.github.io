SpaceSatellite

The Space Satellite allows for wireless communication between machines on earth.

Space Satellite finished structure

To work with satellite, you need to prepare:
- Install Communication Tower

Principle of operation:

After installing the Space Satellite, you must tune the frequency using your Laptop.

Communication Transmitter is used for frequency setting.

Important!

One satellite is enough for the game.
The satellite does not require electrical power.

Setting the frequency for the Space Satellite

To build this multiblock you will need blocks: Space Satellite Casing.
  • Communication Transmitter: 1x
    • The rest of the service hatches are not required.

    Важно!

    The satellite is builded only in orbit or (if orbit is disabled in the GalactiCraft config) on the moon.

    To enable the hologram (hint when building), use the Multiblock Machine Blueprint from the TecTech mod, click it on the multiblock controller.
