Large Steam Turbine

he turbine is capable of generating energy, using steam. Accelerates slowly, used for continuous energy production.

Important!

The functions have been changed compared to the official version of GregTech, please read the information provided carefully.

Large Steam Turbine finished structure

The Large Steam Turbine is powered by steam from GregTech and / or steam from IndustrialCraft 2, producing distilled water in addition to energy.
The turbine requires a rotor to operate. There are many options available, differing in size, material, work efficiency and optimal flow modifier.

Important!

The rotor is disposable, that is, you insert it into the interface of the turbine controller, the turbine remembers the characteristics and remove the rotor, the characteristics are retained until you break the controller.

Efficiency:

Efficiency - percentage influencing turbine energy output.

Optimal flow:

Optimal Flow is the size of the flow required to achieve the optimal energy efficiency of the turbine. Each rotor has an individual optimal flow value.

Fuel Value:

Fuel Value of steam = 0.5 EU/L

Power generation:

nergy generation is performed according to the formula:
EU/t = (Optimal Flow / 20) * Fuel Values * (Efficiency / 100)

An example of generating energy from steam

Large Rotor made of Carbon with an efficiency of 120, with an optimal flow of 3000 L/s.

Power generation:
EU/t = (3000 / 20) * 0.5 * (120 / 100) = 90 EU/t.

To build this multiblock you will need blocks the following: Turbine Casing.
  • Dynamo Hatch (Back center): 1x
  • Maintenance Hatch (Side center): 1x
  • Input Hatch for steam (Side center): 1x
  • Output Hatch for dist. water (Side center): 1x
    • The rest of the service hatches are not required.

    To enable the hologram (hint when building), use the Multiblock Machine Blueprint from the TecTech mod, click it on the multiblock controller.
