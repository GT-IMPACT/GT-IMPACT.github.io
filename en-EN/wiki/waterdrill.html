Electric Water Drilling Rig

Designed for pumping water from the bedrock using electricity.

Electric Water Drilling Rig finished structure

The machine pumps out water from the bedrock using electricity, depending on the biome in which the controller is located, and Energy Hatch and Output Hatch range.

Water generation coefficient:


Water generation coefficient - h. The coefficient is changed using the Energy Hatch and Output Hatch range.

Biome Coefficient:


Biome coefficient - b. The coefficient is changed using the biome in which the controller is located:
  • Ocean, River: b = 1000 L/s
  • Swamplant: b = 800 L/s
  • Jungle: b = 350 L/s
  • Snow, Iceland: b = 300 L/s
  • Plains, Forest: b = 250 L/s
  • Taiga: b = 175 L/s
  • Beach: b = 170 L/s
  • Other: b = 100 L/s

    • Water generation:


    Water is generated at two coefficients according to the following formula:
    Water generation (L/s) = (1 * 2 ^ h) * 2 * b

    To build this multiblock you will need blocks: Solid Steel Machine Casing и Steel Frame Box.
  • Energy Hatch: 1x
  • Maintenance Hatch: 1x
  • Output Hatch: 1x
    • The rest of the service hatches are not required.

    To enable the hologram (hint when building), use the Multiblock Machine Blueprint from the TecTech mod, click it on the multiblock controller.

    Important!

    Energy Hatch and Output Hatch must have the same tier.

    *Do not forget to put the Mining pipe in the controller slot, or in the additional Input Bus.
