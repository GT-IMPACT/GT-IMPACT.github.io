Coke Oven

This machine is designed for the production of charcoal / coke coal and creosote in the early stages.

Coke Oven finished structure

The oven carries out recipes that can be seen at NEI.
This machine has no unusual mechanics. An important note - all recipes are completed in the same time: 90 seconds.

Wood coking

Coal coking

To build this multiblock you will need blocks: Coke Oven Brick.
  • Primitive Output Hatch x1
  • Primitive Output Bus x1
  • Primitive Input Bus x1

    • The service hatches have the same texture as the Coke Oven Brick.
    The rest of the service hatches are not required.

    *Don't forget to enable multiblock with Soft Mallet

    To enable the hologram (hint when building), use the Multiblock Machine Blueprint from the TecTech mod, click it on the multiblock controller.
